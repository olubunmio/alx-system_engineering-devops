A post mortem report is made up of five parts:
1. An issue summary
2. A timeline
3. Root cause analysis
4. Resolution and recovery
5. Corrective and preventative measures.
Lets review each of these parts in detail.


Issue Summary
- Short summary (5 sentences)
- List the duration along with start and end times (including timezone)
- State the impact (e.g `most user requests resulted in 500 errors, at peak 100%`)
- Close with root cause


Timeline
- List the timezone
- Covers the outage duration
- When outage began
- When staff was notified
- Actions, events, ...
- When service was restored


Root Cause Analysis
- Give a detailed explanation of event.
- Do not sugarcoat.


Resolution and Recovery
- Give detailed explanation of actions taken (includes times).


Corrective and Preventive Measures
- Itemized lists of ways to prevent it from happenning again
- What can we do better next time?
